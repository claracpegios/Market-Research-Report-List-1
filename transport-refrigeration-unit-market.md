<p><p>The transport refrigeration unit market comprises three key types: truck, van, and trailer refrigeration systems. Truck refrigeration units are installed within heavy-duty vehicles, allowing for the transport of large quantities of perishable goods over long distances. Van refrigeration units are used in smaller delivery vans, ideal for urban logistics and shorter routes. Trailer refrigeration units are standalone systems attached to trailers, offering flexibility for transporting temperature-sensitive cargo across diverse settings. Each type is essential for maintaining the integrity of refrigerated goods during transit.</p></p>
