<p><ul><li>Single Level Ventilators</li><li>Bilevel Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home non-invasive ventilators are essential for patients with respiratory issues, focusing on two main types: single-level and bilevel ventilators. Single-level ventilators deliver a consistent airflow at a fixed pressure, ideal for patients with stable respiratory conditions. In contrast, bilevel ventilators provide two different pressure levels: one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation, accommodating patients with varying breathing patterns, especially during sleep. Both types enhance patient comfort and support respiratory function in home care settings.</p></p>
