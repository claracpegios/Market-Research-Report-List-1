The squeezing method involves mechanically pressing flaxseeds to extract oil, preserving its nutritional integrity and flavor. In contrast, the leaching method uses solvents to dissolve and extract oil, often yielding higher quantities but potentially compromising quality. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences based on taste, nutritional value, and production efficiency, influencing market dynamics and pricing.</p></p>
