<p><ul><li>Point Sample Test Based Glucose Monitors</li><li>CGM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diabetes monitor market comprises primarily two types: point sample test-based glucose monitors and continuous glucose monitors (CGM). Point sample test-based monitors provide a quick snapshot of blood glucose levels through fingerstick tests, ideal for periodic checks. In contrast, CGMs continuously track glucose levels through a sensor placed under the skin, offering real-time data and trends, enhancing diabetes management. Both types cater to varying patient needs, driving innovation and improving outcomes in diabetes care.</p></p>
