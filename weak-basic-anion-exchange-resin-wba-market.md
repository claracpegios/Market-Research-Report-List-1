<p><p>Weak Basic Anion Exchange Resin (WBA) is a type of ion exchange resin that is used for removing anions from aqueous solutions. Unlike strong basic anion exchange resins, WBA resins operate effectively at lower pH levels, making them suitable for various applications, such as in water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage industries. The unique ability of these resins to selectively adsorb certain anions while allowing others to pass through has led to their increasing adoption in various sectors.</p><p>Market growth for Weak Basic Anion Exchange Resin is projected to be robust, with an expected CAGR of 8.6% during the forecast period.
