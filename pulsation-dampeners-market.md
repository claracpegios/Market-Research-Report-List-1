<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Bladder Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulsation dampeners market features two primary types: diaphragm type and bladder type. Diaphragm type dampeners utilize a flexible membrane to absorb pressure fluctuations, effectively reducing system vibrations. They are ideal for applications requiring precise fluid handling. In contrast, bladder type dampeners incorporate a flexible bladder filled with gas, allowing for greater adaptability to varying pressures. This design excels in applications with significant pulsation and pressure variations, ensuring smoother operation in hydraulic and pneumatic systems across various industries.</p></p>
