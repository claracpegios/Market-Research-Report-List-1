<p><p>Gamma Glutamyl Transferase (GGT) testing is crucial for diagnosing liver and biliary disorders. Two primary market types include ELISA-based and colorimetric-based methods. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) offers high specificity and sensitivity, making it suitable for quantifying GGT levels in various samples. In contrast, colorimetric methods rely on visible light absorption changes to detect GGT activity, often providing rapid results at lower costs. Both approaches cater to different healthcare settings, from clinical laboratories to point-of-care testing.</p></p>
