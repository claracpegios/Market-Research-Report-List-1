<p><p>A cartridge pool filter is a type of filtration system used in swimming pools to remove impurities and debris from the water. Unlike sand or diatomaceous earth filters, cartridge filters utilize a cylindrical cartridge made of pleated fabric to capture contaminants. They are known for their efficiency, ease of maintenance, and ability to filter out finer particles, making them a popular choice for both residential and commercial pools.</p><p>The Cartridge Pool Filter Market is experiencing significant growth, fueled by the increasing demand for improved water quality and the rising number of swimming pools worldwide.
