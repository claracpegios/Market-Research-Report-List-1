<p><p>The printed leather market is divided into two main types: real leather and synthetic leather. Real leather is made from animal hides, valued for its durability, natural texture, and breathability, making it a premium choice for high-end products. In contrast, synthetic leather, often made from polyurethane or PVC, mimics the appearance of real leather at a lower cost. It offers advantages like easier maintenance and ethical production, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers. Both types serve diverse applications across fashion, upholstery, and accessories.</p></p>
