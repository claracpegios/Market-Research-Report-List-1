<p><p>The explosion-proof electrical equipment market comprises various types designed to ensure safety in hazardous environments. Flame-proof types contain explosions within their housings, while increased safety types minimize ignition risks through enhanced design. Intrinsic safety types limit energy to prevent ignition. Positive-pressure types use pressurized air to keep combustible materials at bay, while oil-immersed types use oil to cool and suppress potential ignition. Sand-filled types utilize sand to absorb energy, and other types include specialized solutions tailored to specific needs or environments.</p></p>
