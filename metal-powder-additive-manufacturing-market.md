<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing encompasses techniques like Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM), among others. SLM uses a high-power laser to selectively melt metal powder layer by layer, creating complex geometries with high detail and density. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum, suitable for high-performance materials, particularly titanium alloys. Other market types may include binder jetting and direct energy deposition, each offering unique benefits for various industrial applications.</p></p>
