<p><p>The Automatic Static Retorts market comprises three main types: Steam and Water Spray Retorts, Water Immersion Retorts, and Air Steam Retorts. Steam and Water Spray Retorts utilize a combination of steam and water for even heat distribution, ensuring effective sterilization. Water Immersion Retorts submerge products in heated water, offering consistent temperature control. Air Steam Retorts use a mix of air and steam circulation, promoting efficient heat transfer and uniform processing. Each type caters to different food preservation and safety requirements in the food processing sector.</p></p>
