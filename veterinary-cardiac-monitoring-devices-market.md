<p><p>Veterinary cardiac monitoring devices include several types that facilitate the assessment of animal heart health. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG recording over 24 to 48 hours, capturing transient arrhythmias. Event recorders, on the other hand, allow for prolonged monitoring by activating when an animal exhibits symptoms, recording episodes intermittently. Additionally, other devices may include telemetry systems and portable ECG machines designed to cater to specific needs and varying animal sizes, enhancing overall cardiac care in the veterinary field.</p></p>
