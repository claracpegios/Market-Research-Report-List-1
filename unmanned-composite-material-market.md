Carbon fiber offers high strength-to-weight ratio and rigidity, making it ideal for aerospace and high-performance applications. Glass fiber, known for its cost-effectiveness and good tensile strength, is widely used in marine and automotive industries. Aramid fiber, recognized for its exceptional impact and abrasion resistance, is crucial in protective gear and military applications. Together, these materials enhance the performance and durability of unmanned systems.</p></p>
