<p><p>The radiofrequency anti-aging skin-tightening device market is primarily categorized into bipolar and monopolar technologies. Bipolar technology utilizes two electrodes positioned close together, delivering focused energy to the treatment area, promoting collagen production and tightening skin with minimal discomfort. In contrast, monopolar technology involves a single electrode positioned further from the treatment site, allowing deeper tissue penetration and broader energy distribution, which can achieve more dramatic results. Both technologies are popular for their non-invasive approach to skin rejuvenation.</p></p>
