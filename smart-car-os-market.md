<p><ul><li>"Real-time OS"</li><li>"Non-real-time OS"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Car OS market is divided into two primary types: real-time operating systems (RTOS) and non-real-time operating systems. RTOS are designed to process data as it comes in, ensuring critical functions like safety and control systems respond instantly, which is crucial for autonomous driving. In contrast, non-real-time OS focuses on tasks that are less time-sensitive, managing infotainment systems and navigation features. Both types are essential for enhancing the overall driving experience and vehicle performance in modern smart cars.</p></p>
