<p><p>Differential pressure sensors are crucial in HVAC applications for monitoring air flow and pressure levels. Digital differential pressure sensors utilize electronic components to provide precise measurements and often feature digital displays for easy reading. They are known for their accuracy and reliability in real-time monitoring. On the other hand, analog differential pressure sensors generate continuous signals proportional to the pressure difference, which can be integrated into existing systems for straightforward monitoring. Both types enhance energy efficiency and system performance in HVAC systems.</p></p>
