<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Simple Matrix</li><li>Active Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Large-Area LCD Display market comprises three primary types: static, simple matrix, and active matrix displays. Static displays show fixed images and are often used for signs and advertisements. Simple matrix displays utilize a grid of pixels to create basic graphics and text, suitable for lower-resolution applications. In contrast, active matrix displays feature individual pixel control, enabling high resolutions and fast refresh rates, making them ideal for advanced applications such as smartphones and high-definition televisions.</p></p>
