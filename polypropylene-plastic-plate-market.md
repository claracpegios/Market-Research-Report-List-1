<p><p>The polypropylene plastic plate market is primarily divided into two types: extruded polypropylene and cast polypropylene. Extruded polypropylene is produced by forcing molten plastic through a die, resulting in a continuous sheet that is often used for packaging and engineering applications due to its strength and versatility. In contrast, cast polypropylene is made by pouring liquid polymer into molds, leading to sheets with superior clarity and dimensional stability, making it ideal for items requiring intricate designs and high-quality surfaces.</p></p>
