<p><p>The soluble tapioca fiber market comprises two main types: soluble tapioca fiber syrup and soluble tapioca fiber powder. Soluble tapioca fiber powder, on the other hand, is a dehydrated form used as a dietary fiber supplement and to improve the fiber content in baked goods and snacks. Both types offer health benefits such as improved gut health and reduced sugar content.</p></p>
