<p><ul><li>Line Resist</li><li>Space Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF dry resist market is categorized into line resist and space resist types. Line resist is utilized for defining fine patterns, enabling precise feature delineation in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, space resist focuses on the creation of gaps or spaces between lines, facilitating component separation and improving circuit performance. Both types are crucial for advancing photolithography processes in the electronics industry, optimizing resolution and enabling the production of smaller, more efficient devices.</p></p>
