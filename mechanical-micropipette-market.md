<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical micropipette market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel micropipettes are designed for precise fluid handling in individual samples, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy and ease of use. In contrast, multi-channel micropipettes allow simultaneous transfer of multiple samples, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput experiments like PCR or plate assays. Both types cater to different laboratory needs, with single-channel models focusing on precision and multi-channel models prioritizing speed and productivity in sample processing.</p></p>
