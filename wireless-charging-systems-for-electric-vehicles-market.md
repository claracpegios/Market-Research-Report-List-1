<p><p>Wireless charging systems for electric vehicles (EVs) can be categorized into two main types: dynamic and stationary systems. Dynamic wireless charging systems enable EVs to charge while in motion, utilizing embedded road technology to transfer power as the vehicle drives over charging pads. In contrast, stationary wireless charging systems allow EVs to charge when parked over a designated charging pad, providing convenience and reducing the need for physical plugs. Both systems enhance the EV charging experience through improved efficiency and user convenience.</p></p>
