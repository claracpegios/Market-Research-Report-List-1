<p><strong>The Automotive UV Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UVA</li><li>UVB</li><li>UVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive UV sensors market is categorized into three types based on their wavelength: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA sensors detect long-wave UV radiation, primarily affecting skin aging and surface materials in vehicles. UVB sensors measure medium-wave UV radiation, linked to sunburn and more intense harm to surfaces. UVC sensors, although less common in automotive applications, monitor short-wave UV radiation, often used for disinfecting purposes. Together, these sensors enhance vehicle safety and interior longevity by providing UV exposure data.</p></p>
