<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 serology testing market encompasses various testing types. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results using a simple procedure, making them suitable for widespread screening. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISAs) offer higher sensitivity and specificity, often used in clinical settings for detailed antibody quantification. Neutralization Assays measure the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, providing insight into immune response and vaccine efficacy. Each method serves distinct roles in understanding and managing COVID-19 immunity and infection rates.</p></p>
