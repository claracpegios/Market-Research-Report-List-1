<p><ul><li>TO Case</li><li>Flat Case</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Case for SMT Packaging market encompasses two primary types: TO (Transistor Outline) cases and Flat cases. TO cases are typically cylindrical or rectangular enclosures designed for mounting discrete semiconductor devices, providing excellent thermal performance and electrical insulation. Flat cases, on the other hand, are used for surface-mount technology (SMT) components, featuring a low profile for efficient space utilization on printed circuit boards. Both types ensure reliability and protection for electronic components in various applications.</p></p>
