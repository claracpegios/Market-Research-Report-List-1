<p><p>The construction dumper market primarily comprises three types: articulated dump trucks, rigid dump trucks, and others. Articulated dump trucks feature a hinge between the cab and the cargo bed, allowing for better maneuverability on rough terrain. Rigid dump trucks have a fixed chassis, offering higher capacity and stability, ideal for large-scale operations. The 'others' category includes specialized vehicles like low-profile trucks and mini-dumpers, which cater to niche applications and varying site conditions, enhancing operational flexibility.</p></p>
