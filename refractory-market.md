<p><strong>The Refractory Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory market is divided into two primary types: shaped and unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories include pre-formed products like bricks and tiles, designed for specific applications in furnaces and kilns, offering precise dimensions and structural integrity. Unshaped refractories, or monolithic refractories, consist of materials like castables and ramming mixes, which can be molded into various shapes on-site. Both types are essential for high-temperature environments, providing thermal stability and resistance to chemical attack, crucial for industries such as metallurgy and ceramics.</p></p>
