Copper absorber plates are valued for their excellent thermal conductivity and durability, making them highly efficient but more expensive. Aluminum absorber plates offer a balance of performance and cost, being lightweight and resistant to corrosion, suitable for various applications. Steel absorber plates, while generally the least expensive, are heavier and less efficient, often used in budget-conscious projects. Each type serves different market needs.</p></p>
