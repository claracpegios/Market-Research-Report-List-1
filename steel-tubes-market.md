<p><ul><li>Welded Tube</li><li>Seamless Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel tubes market is primarily divided into two categories: welded tubes and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are created by fusing metal strips or plates, making them more cost-effective for large volumes, though their strength is often lower than seamless options. Seamless tubes are manufactured without joints, offering superior strength and resistance to pressure, commonly used in critical applications like oil and gas. Each type serves distinct industries, impacting their demand based on specific engineering and structural needs.</p></p>
