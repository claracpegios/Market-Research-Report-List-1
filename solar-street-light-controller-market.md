<p><strong>The Solar Street Light Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MPPT Controller</li><li>PWM Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar street light controller market consists primarily of two types: MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controllers and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) controllers. MPPT controllers optimize energy harvesting from solar panels by adjusting the electrical operating point, thus enhancing efficiency, particularly in variable light conditions. In contrast, PWM controllers regulate charging and discharging by switching on and off, offering a simpler, cost-effective solution for smaller installations. Both types play crucial roles in maximizing solar energy usage for street lighting applications.</p></p>
