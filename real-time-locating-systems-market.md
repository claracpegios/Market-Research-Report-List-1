<p><p>Real-Time Locating Systems (RTLS) utilize various technologies to track and manage assets in real time. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) uses tags and readers for short-range tracking, ideal for inventory management. Wi-Fi-based systems leverage existing networks for locating devices within larger areas. UWB (Ultra-Wideband) offers high precision tracking over short distances, making it suitable for applications requiring accuracy. GPS (Global Positioning System) provides location data over vast ranges, commonly used for outdoor tracking of vehicles and personnel. Each technology serves distinct market needs.</p></p>
