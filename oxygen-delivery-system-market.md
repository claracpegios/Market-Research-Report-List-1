<p><p>The oxygen delivery system market encompasses various types of devices designed to provide supplemental oxygen to patients. Oxygen masks cover the nose and mouth for effective delivery, while nasal cannulas deliver oxygen through two small tubes in the nostrils. Venturi masks allow precise oxygen concentration control, and non-rebreather masks prevent exhaled air from mixing with delivered oxygen. Bag valve masks are used for emergency ventilation, while CPAP masks are designed for continuous airway pressure. Other devices include nebulizers and oxygen tanks.</p></p>
