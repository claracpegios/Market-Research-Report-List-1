<p><p>The Oil & Gas Biocides Market is categorized into two main types: oxidizing fungicides and non-oxidizing fungicides. Oxidizing fungicides function by disrupting cellular processes through oxidation, effectively eliminating a wide range of microorganisms, including fungi and bacteria. Non-oxidizing fungicides, on the other hand, inhibit microbial growth through various biochemical mechanisms without oxidation. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity of oil and gas infrastructure by preventing biofouling and ensuring operational efficiency in production and pipeline systems.</p></p>
