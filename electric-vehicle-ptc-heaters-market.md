<p><p>The Electric Vehicle PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) heaters market includes three main types: immersion heaters, radiator heaters, and pad style heaters. Immersion heaters directly heat coolant, providing efficient temperature regulation within the battery or cabin. Radiator heaters distribute heated air through a vehicle's cabin, enhancing passenger comfort. Pad style heaters provide a flexible heating solution, often installed on battery packs or components to ensure optimal operating temperatures. Collectively, these heating systems improve driver comfort and vehicle efficiency in electric vehicles.</p></p>
