<p><p>Second-hand shopping apps primarily operate in two market types: B2C (Business-to-Consumer) and C2C (Consumer-to-Consumer). In the B2C model, businesses sell pre-owned items directly to consumers, often providing a curated selection and assurance of quality. In contrast, the C2C model facilitates peer-to-peer transactions, where individuals buy and sell items among themselves, fostering community engagement and often resulting in better deals. Both models enhance sustainability by promoting reuse and reducing waste in consumer culture.</p></p>
