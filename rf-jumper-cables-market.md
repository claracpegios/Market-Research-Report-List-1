<p><p>The RF jumper cables market is segmented into three main types based on connector gender. Male-to-Male jumpers feature connectors on both ends, commonly used to connect two female ports. Male-to-Female jumpers have a male connector on one end and a female connector on the other, facilitating connections between devices with differing ports. Female-to-Female jumpers, with connectors on both ends, are used to link two male connectors. Each type serves specific applications in telecommunications and electronics, ensuring versatile connections.</p></p>
