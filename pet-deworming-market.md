<p><strong>The Pet Deworming Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Deworming</li><li>External Deworming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pet deworming market is primarily divided into two types: internal and external deworming. Internal deworming focuses on the treatment of parasites inside the pet's body, such as intestinal worms and roundworms, typically using oral or injectable medications. In contrast, external deworming targets parasites residing on the pet's skin, like fleas and ticks, often employing topical treatments or collars. Both types are essential for maintaining pet health and preventing the transmission of parasites to humans and other animals.</p></p>
