<p><p>The wet electric shavers market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and foil shavers. Rotary shavers feature circular blades that move in a circular motion, adapting easily to the contours of the face, making them ideal for those with thicker hair or varied facial shapes. In contrast, foil shavers utilize oscillating blades beneath a thin foil layer, providing a closer shave and better suited for fine or straight hair types. Both types cater to different preferences and shaving needs.</p></p>
